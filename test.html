System Announcements

  • Drops showing as "unavailable" (Tuesday, August 22, 2017)

    Unavailable Students in Roster and Grade Center
    Due to changes in Federal Financial Aid policy, students who drop courses will remain on Blackboard rosters and Grade Center as Unavailable.  These students do not have access to course materials, but will appear in the Users and Rosters tools and the Grade Center.   UNO Faculty Senate approved this change in Spring 2017.
